# PokeLang

### Keywords
* ```if        | eevee      | ^(eevee)$```
* ```elif      | leafeon    | ^(leafeon)$```
* ```else      | espeon     | ^(espeon)$```
* ```for       | forretress | ^(forretress)$```
* ```while     | poliwhirl  | ^(poliwhirl)$```
* ```functions | pokegym    | ^(pokegym)$```
* ```return    | pokecenter | ^(pokecenter)$```
* ```print     | pokedex    | ^(pokedex)$```
* ```int       | porygon    | ^(porygon)$```
* ```float     | porygon2   | ^(porygon2)$```
* ```bool      | disguise   | ^(disguise)$```
* ```string    | unown      | ^(unown)$```

### Integer
* ```^(-|\+)?\d+$```

### Floating Point
* ```^(-|\+)?[0-9]\d*(.\d+)?$```

### Boolean
* ```^(True|False|0|1)```

### String
* ```^"[\s\S]*"$```

### Identifiers
* ```^([a-zA-Z])+(\w)*```

### Operators
* ```+     | plusle    | ^\+$```
* ```-     | minun     | ^\-$```
* ```*     | dialga    | ^\*$```
* ```/     | palkia    | ^\/$```
* ```=     | mew       | ^=$```
* ```==    | mewtwo    | ^(==)$```
* ```&&    | girafarig | ^(&&)$```
* ```||    | farigiraf | ^(||)$```
* ```\n    | spoink    | ^\\n$```
